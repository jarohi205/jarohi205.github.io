The Department of Photography

BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa Campus

Contact us

All the events on campus ranging from alumni talks to fests such as Waves are covered by The Department of Photography. A team of more than 150 members work hard to click stunning pictures and produce amazing aftermovies and videos throughout the year.

All the fests like Zephyr, Coalesence, Waves, TEDx, Quark, Spree are covered by DoPy with full photo and video coverage along with aftermovies which makes these fests last forever in heart of every individual.

Along with these fests all the talks, guest lectures,Farewell Covocation are covered by The Department of photography.Basically if you see any event happening on campus, you'll find DoPy covering it.
